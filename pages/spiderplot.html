Spiderplot Compare

Select up to four exoplanets and compare their normalized orbital, planetary, and stellar values in one shared spiderplot.

Choose 1 to 4 planets. Search only shows planets with at least 3 known values.

Compare uses one shared scale mode for every axis.

Axis Controls

Toggle axes on or off. Drag metrics or use Up/Down to change their order around the spiderplot.

How to Read

Data Point Exact known value for a metric.
Solid Line Connects contiguous known values.
Dashed Area Spans across metrics where data is missing.
