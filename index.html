NASA Exoplanet Archive

Exoplanet Visualization

Explore the dataset through coordinated views that reveal how planets differ by orbit, size, distance, discovery year, and stellar properties.

How to use this app

Each view is built for a different kind of comparison. Use the parallel coordinate plot for many dimensions at once, the spiderplot for compact comparisons, and the scatterplot for focused two-variable analysis.

Main Views

Parallel Coordinate Plot Inspect many exoplanet fields at once and filter by dimension. Spiderplot Compare normalized exoplanet metrics in a radial view. Spiderplot Gallery Browse individual spiderplots for specific planets. Scatterplot Explore relationships between two selected variables.

Interactive Labs

Habitable Zone Explorer Discover where liquid water could exist based on star properties. Gravity Drop Simulator Experience and compare the surface gravity on different exoplanets. Transit Light Curve Lab See how planets are discovered by watching their transit light curves.

Planet Selectors

Planet Selector Build a custom dataset of exoplanets for the visualizations.
